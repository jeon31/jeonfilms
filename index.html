About

Who we are

Add portrait here

We believe every frame is a chance to say something true.

Jeon Films is a creative studio founded by Jivan — originally from Nepal, now based in Utah, United States. With over five years of hands-on experience across the full spectrum of visual production, we bring craft, intention, and a cross-cultural perspective to every project.

We work across video editing, cinematography, animation, and visual effects — short films, branded content, commercial work, and client productions. Our process is focused: we find the story, then build everything around it.

Whether shaping raw footage or building from nothing, we give it everything we have.
