I'm Jivan, a filmmaker, video editor, and VFX artist originally from Nepal, now based in the United States.

My background spans film editing, VFX compositing, motion graphics, animation, and live event cinematography — built through years of professional work at top production houses including DishHome and Jazz Production.

I grew up telling stories through visuals before I ever knew what a frame rate was. That instinct drives every cut, every composite, every frame I touch.

Currently studying filmmaking in the U.S., I bring a cross-cultural perspective to every project — understanding how stories land differently across audiences, and crafting visuals that transcend language.
