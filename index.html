About Me

The filmmaker

I'm Jivan, a filmmaker, video editor, and VFX artist originally from Nepal — now based in Utah, United States, studying filmmaking full-time.

My professional journey spans film editing, VFX compositing in Nuke, motion graphics, animation, and live event cinematography — with credits at DishHome, Jazz Production, and an e-learning company back in Nepal.

Growing up in a village, adapting to city life, then crossing continents — that cross-cultural perspective lives in every project I touch. I understand how stories land differently across languages, cultures, and screens.

"I don't just edit footage. I shape the feeling between the frames."
