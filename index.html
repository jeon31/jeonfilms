About

Who we are

We believe every frame is a chance to say something true.

Jeon Films is a creative studio founded by Jivan, originally from Nepal and now based in Utah, United States. With over five years of hands-on experience in visual production, we bring craft, intention and a cross-cultural perspective to every project we take on.

We work across video editing, cinematography, animation and visual effects. From short films and commercials to branded content and client productions, we always lead with the story and let the craft follow.

Whether we are shaping raw footage or starting from nothing, we give it everything we have.
